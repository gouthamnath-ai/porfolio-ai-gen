About Me

Behind the Screen

I'm Goutham Nath, an AI Generalist based in Kerala, India. I work across the full creative pipeline — writing prompts, generating and refining visuals, editing reels, and automating the workflows that hold it all together.

My work sits at the intersection of AI tools and real-world business needs — from Malayalam-language social content for local audiences, to internal documentation and ticketing systems for NBFC operations. I like being the person who can move between the creative and the technical without needing a handoff.

What draws me to this field is the pace of it — new tools every month, and the constant question of how to fold them into something a real audience actually responds to. That's the work I want to keep doing.
